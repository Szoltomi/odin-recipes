Description:

Simple, clean fried rice.

The recipe works best when practiced and optimized in small increments.

These are the measurements and exact brands for my own optimized recipe, for one specific pot.

Yours will differ!

Ingredients:

  • 750 ml of water.
  • 400 ml white rice. red Riceland works best, but jasmin rice works too
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sunflower oil

Steps:

  1. Wash the rice, or don't. Dry completely
  2. Add oil to pot, then add the rice
  3. Put some water to heat up. Make it a little hot but nowhere near boiling.
  4. Wait until rice begins lightly sizzling
  5. Begin stirring the rice. On the highest heat stir briskly and continously as it can burn down fast.
  6. The grains will should first turn matte white, then a light straw color. A few brown spots are OK. Black however is yuck.
  7. Add the salt, add the water. Ideally the water is just warm enough to end up below boiling, without mess.
  8. Cover pot, set timer to 18 minutes
  9. Do not stir, do not check
  10. Return seconds before alarm sounds.
    11. Take rice off fire. Stir it up to a fluffy consistency.
  11. Enjoy!
