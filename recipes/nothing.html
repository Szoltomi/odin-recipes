Description:

The opposite of existence

A tricky recipe. Most attempts at making nothing produced something instead

Ingredients:

  • Agentic Conscriousness
  • An inner source of reflection

Steps:

  1. Observe surrounding existence and deduce it's laws
  2. Take the complete set of everything, then subtract itself from itself.
  3. If the result is tanglible, previous step was not followed accurately. Repeat #2
  4. The universe should be no different, but the concept is born in the mind
  5. Enjoy the formless void given immaterial lack of existence
