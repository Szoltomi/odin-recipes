Cake

A simple but involved recipe

May involve metaphysics

To make a cake, as one sometimes says, it's required to make the universe at first.

Ingredients:

  • An empty spaceless void
  • A permeating active consciousness

Steps:

  1. Using your everbearing powers, cause random quantum fluctuations in the volumeless ether
  2. Observe existence coming to being
  3. Guide the infant multiverse into complete productive branching
  4. Explore resulting infinite, infinitely expanding set of universes for recipes of Cake
  5. Gather ingredients for your chosen type of Cake
  6. Find a suitable location, and prepare your Cake according to the instructions
  7. enjoy!
